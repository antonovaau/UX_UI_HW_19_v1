    My Work

    Non-profit RWD UI Redesign for “Save-A-Dog”

    A shared bond over animals and a poorly designed current website led us to choose the Save-A-Dog website for a redesign. We redesigned Save-A Dog website become a driver in pushing adoptions for homeless pets and donations by increasing its appeal and improving usability on the site.

    “ABC” website redesign

    When visiting “ABC” website you would expect this to be perfect: well structured, with fair, accurate and inclusive information. However, we discovered that there was room for an improvement. WRD redesign deliver improved user experience browsing, structured and consistent navigation, organised articles layout.

    UX Case Study for mobile app “Plant Pal”

    Plant Pal was designed to help users manage their plants by taking into account their living spaces, and helping with watering schedules by notifying users to take care of their plants while also informing them on the relevant information regarding their plant.
